game Rules

  • First team to press the button has 3 seconds to answer.
  • Only the person who pressed the button can answer, not the whole team.
  • If the first team did not answer in 3 seconds, the other team gets 10 seconds to answer.
  • If the second team did not answer in 10 seconds, the question will be open for all.
  • Each team must capture a continuous line of cells horizontally or vertically based on their color.
